Chapter Six - The Last Duel


With the strength from the concoction, you fully regained your strength and made your way to the peak of the mountain. You survey the area for the large tree that houses the jewel you seek. Nothing. You regard the map closely and look up again to where the tree should be and now you notice splintered wood scattering the ground. “Boy!” You hear a familiar gruff voice. “I have already harvested the jewel you seek. You have no hope of wielding a blade superior to a renowned blacksmith” he grinned devilishlily. “”If you truly follow the black demon, none are safe. I can’t let you live” you yell.
